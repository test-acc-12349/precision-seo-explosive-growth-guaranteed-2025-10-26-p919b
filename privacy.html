Cookies and Similar Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and enhance your experience with our Services. We use both session-based and persistent cookies.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality of our Services, such as authentication and security
  • • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how you use our Services and measure performance
  • • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to personalize your experience
  • • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity across websites for targeted advertising purposes

Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use other tracking technologies such as:

  • • Web Beacons: Small graphic images that track page visits and usage patterns
  • • Pixels: Invisible tags that track conversions and user interactions
  • • Local Storage: Technology that stores data on your device similar to cookies

Third-Party Analytics

We use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to help us understand how visitors use our Services. These services may collect information about your browsing activities across different websites. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
